Mug Brownie Recipe

Learn how to make an easy mug brownie as a fun dessert!

Prep Time: 5 mins | Cook Time: 5 mins | Total Time: 10 mins | Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • ¼ cup all-purpose flour
  • ¼ cup white sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa powder
  • 1 pinch ground cinnamon
  • 1 pinch salt
  • ¼ cup water
  • 2 tablespoons canola oil
  • 1 splash vanilla extract

Steps

  1. Stir flour, sugar, cocoa powder, cinnamon, and salt together in a microwave-safe mug. Stir water, oil, and vanilla extract into flour mixture with a fork until smooth.
  2. Microwave until set, 1 to 5 minutes.
